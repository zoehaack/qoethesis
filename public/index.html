User Testing

Welcome to this study!

Please make sure to read the following instructions carefully!

In this experiment, you will watch 5 short video sequences - each around 30 seconds.
After each video you will be asked to rate the video based on your experienced quality while watching.

The total duration of the study will be around 10 minutes, you can track your progress at any time by checking the progress bar at the top of the page

Once you click "Submit" you will be shown an example video to make you familiar with the design and player handling.
After you have watched a video a rating scale and a button will appear. Please rate the video and click the button to continue with the next video.

In order to make sure that the videos play smoothly you have to wait until the progress bar has reached 100%, then you can start the video. The video will start as soon as you click the play button.

Please make sure that you base your rating on your experience and not on the content that you were shown. The rating scale is referenced as follows:

1 2 3 4 5
Bad Poor Fair Good Excellent
